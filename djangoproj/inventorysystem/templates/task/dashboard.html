Recording Delivery on Supply
-For the admin to records all upcoming supplies deliveries the admins must follow the process below
  • Click the “Supplies” dropdown button in your navigation bar
  • Click the “Delivery” button
    • -The admin need to identify if the supply is already recorded or not
  • Input all the information needed in recording
  • Then Click the “Deliver” button
  • Choose the item/supply
  • Click the Green Button “+ADD”
  • Input the quantity that you want to add
  • Then click the “ADD” button

    • The admin can view or accept all the request of other department
  • Select the “Requested Supplies” under the Supplies section in your Navigation bar
  • Select the item you want to accept by clicking the green button “ACCEPT”
  • Input all the information needed in accepting an request
  • Then click the submit button “Accept Request”
    • To notify the other department on the status of their request you need to email them
  • Select an request
  • Click the green button “EMAIL”
  • Click “Email”

    • -After accepting the request and notify other departments and the representative of that department is ready to pick up the supply you may now withdraw the supplies
  • Select the “Withdraw Supplies” under the Supplies section in your Navigation bar
  • Select an request
  • Click the green button “WITHDRAW”
  • Click the button “ok” if all the information are correct

    • -The Admin can limit the amount of supplies for each departments request
  • Click the “Limit Supplies” under the supplies on your navigation bar

  • input all information needed in creating an limit
  • After inputting all the information needed click the “submit” button
  • Choose an limit
  • Click the "green button" update
  • Input the quantity that you want to add
  • then Click the submit button "ADD"

    • Equipment

    -The Department must request an equipment first before the supply department order and have an delivery in equipment
  • Select the “Delivery” under the Equipment section in your Navigation bar
  • Select an request
  • Click the green button “DELIVER”
  • Input all the information needed in accepting a request
  • Then click the submit button “Accept Request”
    • -To notify the other department on the status of their request you need to email them
  • Select an request
  • Click the green button “EMAIL”
  • Click the submit button “Email”

  • Select the “Delivery Records” under the Equipment section in your Navigation bar
    • -The admin can view here all the records in delivery of equipment. Also the admin can save/convert the record in pdf or xls format by clicking on the two blue button “Convert to PDF” and “Export to XLS”

    -Before withdrawing the equipment, ICS or ARE are a must upon transaction.

    Fill inputs such as department and date

    when the equipment is accepted.
  • Click “Create Form” button
    • Complete the necessary information in creating the form
  • Click “Save” button
  • Click “Create Form” Button

    • -Fill inputs such as department and date when the equipment is accepted.
  • Click “Create Form” button
    • -Complete the necessary information in creating the form
  • Click “Save” button
  • Click “Create Form” Button

    • -After the equipment is delivered and the arrival of the representative of the department who requested it, the admin may/can now withdraw the equipment.

  • Select the “Withdrawn Equipments” under the Equipment section in your Navigation bar
  • Click on the Green button “WITHDRAW”
  • Input all the information needed in Withdrawing an Equipment
  • Then click the submit button “Create QR”
  • Save it to your computer
  • After saving the QR code
  • You may now withdraw the equipment

  • Select the “Delivery” under the Equipment section in your Navigation bar
  • The admin will scan the QR attache in the equipment
  • And the click the return button
  • The equipment will show below after returning it
  • The admin can dispose it by clicking the green button “DISPOSE”

    • Storage Mapping
  • Select the “Storage Mapping” in your Navigation menu
    • -All supply and their location will be shown in the storage mapping

    -The admin can update the location of each supply in the storage mapping
  • Select an supply and then click the green button “UPDATE”
  • Input all the information needed
  • Click the submit button “UPDATE”

    • Add Admin Account
    -After you login and have access to the website you may now create your own or staff account by registering it.
  • Click the “Add Admin Account” in your Navigation Bar
  • Fill in the details needed (Username, Email, Password)
  • Retype your password
  • Click the “REGISTER” Button
    • -After you click the register button you must have this notification
  • Press Ok then you can now logout and login your new account

    • Add Department Account
    -To allow the other departments to have limited access offered by TUP supply inventory system, the admin should register their account by registering it.
  • Click the “Add Department Account” in your Navigation Bar
  • Fill in the details needed (Username, Email, Department Name, Password)
  • Retype your password
  • Click the “REGISTER” Button
    • -Every department will receive an email confirmation from the email address that the supply admin has registered, meaning that their account is now accessible.
